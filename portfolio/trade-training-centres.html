Trade Training Centres

Western Australia
Armstrong Parkin Architects, 2009-12

The design of Trade Training Centres in secondary schools requires that the needs of a variety of stakeholders are met, including the subject teachers, school principal, TAFE trainers, Department of Education, and the funding agency. At Armstrong Parkin Architects Kate provided design, documentation and contract administration services on trade training centres for Hospitality and Catering, Carpentry, Metalwork, and Light Automotive.
