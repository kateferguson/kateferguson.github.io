Services

Kate is an architect specialising in participatory design process for community development.

Community Building Projects

These projects are community-building in the sense that there is a focus on people working together, defining a challenge, sharing ideas and inspiration, creating a plan and achieving their goals. They are also building projects, in the sense that there is a physical outcome.

Project Planning and Mobilisation

Assistance to define your project goals, decide whom you need to engage with, work out what funding you require and help you to source it.

Promotion

Strategies, communication and design work to reach the people who need to hear about the project.

Participatory Design

Strategies and activities to gather design ideas, analyse benefits and drawbacks and refine a solution.

Project Management

Assistance with keeping the project organised, progessing, and on budget.

Mentoring

Individual or group mentoring for people in your community who want to develop specific skills. This can be done formally, such as meetings that combine training and reflection with project work, or informally.

Working Bees

Planning, preparation and facilitation of a safe, fun and successful working bee. This can include safety briefings and instructions for equipment, assigning roles according to abilities, focusing on teamwork, and encouraging a welcoming, supportive atmosphere.

Training

How to deliver a community infrastructure project

This course caters to individuals, civil society organizations and schools who want to set up and deliver a community project. Topics include project planning, stakeholder engagement, partnering, cross-cultural communication, community capacity building, participatory design, prototyping, culturally and environmentally appropriate design, construction planning, monitoring and evaluation.

Participants work in small groups through case studies and practical exercises. The workshops focus on developing a collegiate atmosphere, and it is hoped participants will continue to support each other and share information as they move their projects forward.

Participants receive a copy of the Participatory Design Handbook, along with booklet of further resources such as books, articles, websites and video.

Customised Training

Kate can develop a training course to specifically meet the needs of your group.

Architectural Services

Kate is a registered architect in Victoria. Kate focuses on the early stages of brief development, stakeholder and community engagement and participatory design

Brief Development

Engagement of stakeholders in accessible and appealing ways, allowing them to contribute meaningfully to the development of an architectural brief. Experience in engagement, facilitation, report-writing and architecture make Kate well suited to be the connector between stakeholders and an architect.

Participatory Design

Participatory Design is a methodology that enables users of the building to contribute to its design. It can be useful as part of brief development to explore priorities, relationships between spaces, circulation flow, and atmosphere.

Schematic Design

As an awarded architectural designer, Kate can develop options for your development that meet the needs of different stakeholders.

Full architectural services

Kate teams up with draftspeople and other architects when full architectural services are required.
